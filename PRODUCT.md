# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Students, parents, and teachers at Patumwan Demonstration School (SWU). Students browse to find department info, faculty profiles, curriculum details, and competition activities. Parents and teachers visit for school resources and contact information. Access is primarily on desktop in a school or home environment.

## Product Purpose

A public-facing school department website for กลุ่มสาระการงานอาชีพและเทคโนโลยี (Technology and Vocational Education Subject Group), Patumwan Demonstration School (SWU). The site presents the department's identity, curriculum, faculty, and resources. Success means students and parents find what they need quickly and feel confident in the department's professionalism.

## Brand Personality

Academic yet modern, trustworthy, accessible.

## Anti-references

Overly corporate enterprise blue, generic Thai government website plainness, flashy gaming-style neon aesthetics.

## Design Principles

1. Clarity first — information hierarchy guides the eye before color does
2. Institutional pride without stuffiness — warm and contemporary, not cold or formal
3. Color with purpose — gradients signal brand identity, not decoration

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Thai-language primary; content must be readable in the Sarabun typeface at all sizes.
